FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve the New York metropolitan area, but we also take on select projects nationwide and internationally for larger residential and commercial projects.

What is your design process?

Our process includes initial consultation, concept development, design refinement, implementation, and final styling. Each phase is collaborative, ensuring your vision is realized.

How much does a project cost?

Project costs vary based on scope, size, and requirements. We offer various service packages and provide detailed proposals after the initial consultation.

How long does a project take?

Project timelines depend on complexity and scope. A single room refresh may take 4-6 weeks, while full home renovations typically take 4-8 months.
