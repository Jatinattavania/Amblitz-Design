About Us

Our Story

A Passion for Design Excellence

Founded in 2014, Amblitz Design began with a simple vision: to create living spaces that inspire, comfort, and stand the test of time. What started as a small studio with just two designers has grown into a full-service luxury interior design firm.

Our founder, driven by a passion for combining classical elegance with modern functionality, set out to redefine luxury interior design. Today, our team of talented designers continues this legacy, crafting bespoke spaces that reflect the unique personalities and lifestyles of our clients.

We believe that great design is not just about aesthetics—it's about creating environments that enhance your daily life, evoke emotions, and tell your story.

How We Work

Our Design Process

A streamlined approach that ensures exceptional results from initial consultation to final reveal.

1. Consultation

We begin with an in-depth conversation to understand your vision, preferences, lifestyle, and budget.

2. Concept Development

Our team creates detailed design concepts, mood boards, and 3D visualizations for your approval.

3. Design Refinement

We refine the designs based on your feedback, selecting materials, finishes, and furnishings.

4. Implementation

Our project managers oversee every detail of the construction and installation process.

5. Styling & Finishing

We add the final touches—art, accessories, and styling—that bring your space to life.

6. Final Reveal

Experience the joy of walking into your beautifully transformed space for the first time.
