Case Study

The Goldstone Residence

Project Overview

The Goldstone Residence is a testament to our commitment to luxury and sophistication. This 5,000 sq ft penthouse in Manhattan required a complete transformation, blending contemporary design with timeless elegance.

The Challenge

Our clients desired a space that would serve as both a sophisticated entertainment venue and a warm family home. The open floor plan needed careful consideration to create distinct yet flowing spaces.

Our Solution

We incorporated rich materials—Italian marble, brass accents, and custom millwork—to create a cohesive design language throughout. Strategic lighting design highlights architectural features while creating intimate moments within the expansive space.

Location: Manhattan, NY
Size: 5,000 sq ft
Duration: 8 months
Style: Contemporary Luxury
